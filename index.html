Teaching Philosophy

My teaching approach emphasizes making complex economic and mathematical concepts accessible through practical applications and real-world examples. I believe in creating an inclusive learning environment where students develop critical thinking skills and understand how theoretical principles apply to contemporary challenges in economics, data analysis, and business. I encourage active participation, collaborative learning, and the practical application of econometric and computational tools to solve meaningful problems.
