Research Projects

Exploring innovative solutions at the intersection of AI, robotics, and human-centered design

2023-2025

Autonomous Navigation Systems for Urban Robots

Developing advanced machine learning algorithms for autonomous robots to navigate complex urban environments safely and efficiently.

MIT, Stanford University

This groundbreaking project focuses on creating robust navigation systems that allow robots to operate seamlessly in dynamic urban settings. Our approach combines computer vision, deep reinforcement learning, and sensor fusion to enable real-time decision-making in crowded, unpredictable environments.

2022-2024

Explainable AI for Healthcare Diagnostics

Creating transparent AI systems that can explain their diagnostic decisions to healthcare professionals, improving trust and adoption.

Johns Hopkins Medical Center

This project addresses one of the most critical challenges in medical AI: interpretability. We develop AI systems that not only provide accurate diagnoses but also explain their reasoning in terms that medical professionals can understand and verify, fostering trust and enabling better patient outcomes.

2021-2023

Natural Language Interfaces for Smart Homes

Building intuitive voice-controlled systems that understand context and user preferences in smart home environments.

Google Research, CMU

This research explores how natural language processing and context awareness can create more intuitive smart home experiences. Our systems learn user preferences over time and understand implicit context, making voice interactions feel more natural and responsive.

2020-2024

Ethical AI Framework Development

Establishing comprehensive frameworks and guidelines for developing AI systems that prioritize fairness, transparency, and accountability.

EU AI Ethics Board

Working with international partners, this project establishes practical frameworks for ethical AI development. We provide guidelines, tools, and methodologies that help researchers and practitioners build AI systems that are fair, transparent, and accountable to diverse stakeholders.

Teaching Philosophy

I believe in fostering a collaborative learning environment where students are encouraged to think critically, ask questions, and explore innovative solutions. My teaching approach combines theoretical foundations with hands-on practical experience, preparing students for real-world challenges in technology and engineering.
